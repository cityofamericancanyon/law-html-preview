19.39.040 Definitions.

"Accessory dwelling unit" means one additional attached or detached residential dwelling unit one thousand two hundred square feet or less that is on the same parcel as a single-family dwelling, and provides permanent and independent provisions for living, sleeping, eating, cooking, and sanitation for one or more persons. An accessory dwelling unit also includes the following:

(1) An efficiency unit, as defined in Section 17958.1 of the Health and Safety Code.

(2) A manufactured home, as defined in Section 18007 of the Health and Safety Code.

"Junior accessory dwelling unit" means one accessory dwelling unit that is five hundred square feet or less that is contained entirely in the primary residence.

"Primary residence" means the residential dwelling that existed on the single-family parcel before or constructed concurrent with the accessory dwelling unit.
